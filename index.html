Who We Are

Forging Elite Athletes
Right here in Western NY

Our mission at Crossfit DunKirk is to tap your hidden potential and illuminate the path to a fulfilling, healthy lifestyle. CrossFit Dunkirk is more than a gym, it’s a community of support, education and encouragement to achieve your fitness goals. You receive expert coaching, individualized attention and stay motivated to work hard and move forward.

Services

Instruction

Each WOD includes a warm-up, personalized instruction on skills and movements, strength work, and a blazing workout to condition you metabolically.

Personal Training

Personal Training is specific one on one Coaching and programming tailored for you and your needs. Each session is individually tailored to your goals and fitness level.

Nutrition Coaching

We work with clients by providing nutrition counseling, customized meal and exercise plans to help them achieve their goals.

Foundations Course

Delivered in small group sessions, these classes are designed to get you to your regular classes with the knowledge you need to be successful.

Our CrossFit program is designed for everyone regardless of experience.

We’ve used our same routines for elderly individuals with heart disease, full-time mom’s, military, police officers, firefighters, people fighting obesity, and those just looking for something different. We’ll modify the movements and intensity to fit your abilities. We have a philosophy when it comes to fitness. There are no shortcuts on the road to success. We believe in teaching people the importance of longevity. There is no such thing as an overnight success.
Our brand of CrossFit is an excellent program that emphasizes the importance of mechanics, consistency, and intensity (in that order). All of our classes are coach led to help guide you to the best hour of your day. Your job is to come in, work hard, have fun and leave happy!
